Short biographical notes

1944 born in Västerås
1965 higher school examination, mathematician
1966-71 graduate engineer education, Chalmers Institute of Technology
1973-76 my first own workshop in Gothenburg
1976-77 built the new workshop and my home in Vansbro, Dalarna
1984-87 freelancer to AB Gustavsberg
1994- glaze consultant to BodaNova - Höganäs Keramik AB
as potter autodidakt
