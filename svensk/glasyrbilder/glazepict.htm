More glaze-pictures: 12-20

Some years ago I made a lot of pictures of my glazes for educational reasons. Somebody gave me the idea to make "art pictures" from the dias. In the last 5 years I have made about 25 different "art pictures" in my colorphoto-lab. Size: 30 x 40 cm. Price: SEK 500 with glass and frame. I make not more than 50 of each. I give the picture a name and sign it. I will show you some of the pictures here.
