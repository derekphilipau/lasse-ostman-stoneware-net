Lasse Östman, potter. The stoneware glaze page

Some of my best and most frequently used glazes. Recipes, tips and sometimes pictures. I make a lot of glaze tests. It must be about ten thousand the last 24 years, mostly oxidation glazes for my electric kilns. The last five years I have also tested many reduction glazes and made several glazes for the ceramic industry.
